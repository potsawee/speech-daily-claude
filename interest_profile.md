# Interest Profile

## Core Interests
- **Speech Synthesis / TTS**: Neural TTS, zero-shot voice cloning, prosody control, expressiveness, multi-speaker synthesis
- **ASR / Speech Recognition**: End-to-end models (conformer, transducer, CTC), streaming ASR, low-resource ASR, multilingual ASR, Whisper-based work
- **Speech Language Models**: LLM-based speech generation/understanding, audio tokens, codec LMs, GPT-style audio models
- **Audio Codec / Tokenization**: Neural audio codecs (EnCodec-style), discrete speech representations, VQ-VAE for audio
- **Voice Conversion**: Zero-shot VC, accent conversion, speaker adaptation
- **Speech Enhancement**: Noise suppression, dereverberation, source separation in real-world conditions
- **Speaker Diarization / Verification**: Who-spoke-when, anti-spoofing, speaker embeddings

## Secondary Interests
- **Music Generation**: Differentiable music synthesis, neural audio generation for music
- **Audio Captioning / Understanding**: Sound event detection, audio question answering, audio-visual learning
- **Self-Supervised Speech**: wav2vec, HuBERT, data2vec for speech
- **Diffusion / Flow Matching for Audio**: Score-based and flow-based generative models applied to speech/audio
- **Multilingual & Low-Resource**: Cross-lingual transfer, code-switching, under-resourced language ASR/TTS

## Ranking Signals (High Priority)
- Novel architecture or training paradigm
- State-of-the-art benchmark results
- Real-time or streaming capability
- Zero-shot/few-shot generalization
- From a top lab or well-known author

## Ranking Signals (Deprioritize)
- Pure survey papers (unless very comprehensive)
- Narrow domain-specific applications unrelated to core speech/audio
- Papers dominated by radar, medical ultrasound, seismic, sonar signal processing
